Strategy Settings

Your trading strategy settings determine how the bot selects and executes covered call trades:

  • Risk Level: Affects strike price selection and premium targets
  • Position Size: Controls maximum investment per stock
  • Profit/Stop: When to take profits or cut losses
  • Expiry: Target days until option expiration
Changes will apply to new trades only. Existing positions will continue with their current settings.
